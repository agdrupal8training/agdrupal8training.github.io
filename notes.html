Drupal 8 Deep Dive Training: Notes

A recap of the requirements for this training:

   
  1. Must have some knowledge of Drupal, Drupal 8 knowledge not required but helpful (have built sites or content types/views)
  2. Must be able to commit to all 5 days of training. (Classes are cumulative)
  3. Need to pay for own parking.
  4. Can bring a mac laptop if they want (requirements for BOYMac below)
 

Requirements for bringing your own laptop (Mac only for byod, we have windows backups there already)

  Acquia dev desktop Installed and configured
D8 Core Installed
Git-bash available at https://git-scm.com/download/win
Sourcetree available at https://www.sourcetreeapp.com/
a visual text editor such as Sublime or an IDE like PHP Storm (something more than a plain text editor at the very least - PHP Storm is especially good for Drupal 8; both Sublime and PHP storm have free evaluation versions)
GoToMeeting to share screens 
public/private key pairs pre-installed so devs can set up individual free Acquia Dev Tier accounts and upload their sites
an installed instance of Drupal 7 for comparative purposes
